Elden Ring is the latest game from FromSoftware which is a Japanse company that made very popolar games such as blodborne and the dark Souls series. It's an open-world role-playing game set in the Lands Between, a devastated region where the destruction of the titular item has resulted in wars and chaos. You take on the role of a warrior entrusted with reclaiming the ring and establishing a new country.In this game you explore and defeat diffuclt bosses by usiing diffrent types of weapons of your choice such as sorcieries or incantations.
