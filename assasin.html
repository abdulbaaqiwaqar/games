Assasin Creed Odesey is one of the best games I played from Ubisoft. It's an open-world rpg game which It tells the mythological storey of Athens and Sparta during the Peloponnesian War, which lasted from 431 to 422 BC. Players take control of a Spartan mercenary who fights on both sides of the conflict in their quest to find their family and destroy the enigmatic Cult of Kosmos.
